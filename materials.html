Be inspired by our wide range of materials

When it comes to creating custom stickers, the material you choose is just as important as the design itself. Vinyl is a popular option for its durability and waterproof properties. For a more eco-friendly and rustic choice opt for Kraft paper which is made out of 100% recycled brown paper. Clear stickers are perfect for adding a subtle touch to any surface or window, and holographic options provide a fun and unique flair. Whatever material you choose it\s easy to showcase your personality and style.
